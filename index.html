    FAQ

    What is NASA Artemis Orbits?

    An interactive 3D viewer for Artemis mission trajectories, event context, and mission-time playback controls.

    Where does the mission data come from?

    From normalized official NASA OEM trajectory data and moon vectors sourced from JPL Horizons.

    How do I share a specific mission moment?

    Use the share controls to copy/post the current URL. It retains mission, camera, and timeline context in query params.
